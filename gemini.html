Gemma: Open-Weights AI Models

Gemma is a family of open-weights models built from the same research and technology used to create the Gemini models.

Get Started

Overview

Gemma models are available in a range of sizes, and are designed to be versatile and accessible for developers and researchers.

Features

Gemma models achieve state-of-the-art performance on a variety of benchmarks.

Gemma is designed to be accessible to a wide range of developers and researchers.

Gemma can be used for a variety of tasks, including text generation, translation, and question answering.
